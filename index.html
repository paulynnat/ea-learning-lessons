Welcome!

This tutorial series will teach you the fundamentals of web development. Each lesson is designed to be:

  • Self-contained and easy to follow
  • Interactive with live examples
  • Filled with hands-on exercises
  • Progressive - building on previous lessons

How to use: Click on each lesson below, open it in your browser, read the instructions, and complete the exercises right in the file!

Tips for Success

  • Complete lessons in order
  • Don't just read - type out the examples yourself
  • Experiment and break things! That's how you learn
  • Complete all exercises before moving to the next lesson
  • Use your browser's Developer Tools (F12) to inspect and debug
